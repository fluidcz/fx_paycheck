## Features

**Job Based Pay** -
Give players a salary based on their current job and grade.

**Custom Pay Interval** -
You decide how often players receive their paycheck.

**Optional Taxes** -
Automatically deduct taxes from each paycheck.

**Anti AFK** -
Advanced anti AFK system to not reward players, that are AFK.

**Multiple Pay Methods**

`bank` - Deposit money directly to the bank.

`cash` - Make players go to the Pacific Bank and claim the money manually.

**Choice of Notification Styles**
Supported: 

`esx`

`ox_lib` 

`lb_phone` (Wallet app)

## Requirements

ESX Framework (`es_extended`)

`ox_lib`

`esx_society` - Optional

## Installation

Download the script.

Put the `fx_paycheck` folder into your `resources` directory.

Open `config.lua` and change the settings to your liking.

Add `ensure fx_paycheck` to your `server.cfg`.

Navigate to `es_extended/shared/config/main.lua`.

On `line 50` set `Config.EnablePaycheck` to `false`.

Restart your server.
